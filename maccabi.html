Maccabi

Maccabi is the second largest health fund in terms of the number of insured patients. It serves more than 2 million people and has its own network of pharmacies with 90 locations throughout the country. Like other health funds, Maccabi has three levels of insurance: Kesef ("Silver"), Zahav ("Gold"), and Sheli ("My Maccabi"). The higher the level, the more medical services are available to patients.
