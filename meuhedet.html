Meuhedet

Meuhedet is the third largest health fund in Israel, serving over a million Israelis. Its main advantage is the shortest waiting times and individual insurance options. For example, Meuhedet offers the "Meuhedet Adif" insurance, which allows customers to receive medical treatment abroad and organ transplantation services.

At the same time, Meuhedet significantly lags behind other health funds in terms of the number of clinics. Despite having over 300 locations throughout the country, Meuhedet does not operate in some cities at all.
