Clalit

Kupat Holim Clalit is the largest health fund in Israel. It was founded in 1911 and serves more than 53% of the country's citizens. The main feature of this health fund is that it conducts serious clinical research and manages offices and medical centers in all populated areas of Israel.

But popularity has its downsides - due to the high demand for this health fund, its patients often face long queues and slow service.
